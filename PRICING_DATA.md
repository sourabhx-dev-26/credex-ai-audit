\# Pricing Logic Notes



The audit engine uses:



\* seat count

\* plan tier

\* estimated usage fit

\* vendor benchmarks



to determine:



\* overspending

\* downgrade opportunities

\* vendor overlap

\* estimated savings



Enterprise pricing varies significantly across organizations. Estimated benchmark pricing was used where vendors do not publicly expose exact enterprise costs.
