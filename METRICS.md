\# METRICS



\# Core Product Metrics



The CREDEX AI Spend Audit product is designed as:



\* a lead generation funnel

\* a SaaS optimization tool

\* a financial insight platform



Success depends not only on traffic, but on:



\* audit completion

\* lead quality

\* conversion intent

\* shareability



\---



\# North Star Metric



\## Qualified Audits Completed



Definition:



\* completed audits from companies likely spending significantly on AI tooling



Why this matters:



\* it combines traffic quality with product engagement

\* reflects real business intent

\* directly correlates with potential monetization



Raw traffic alone is not meaningful if users are not serious buyers.



\---



\# Funnel Metrics



\## 1. Landing Page Conversion Rate



Formula:



```txt id="9gnm9v"

audit\_starts / landing\_page\_visits

```



Tracks:



\* headline effectiveness

\* positioning clarity

\* trust signals

\* onboarding friction



Target:



\* 15–25%



\---



\## 2. Audit Completion Rate



Formula:



```txt id="k9n2ul"

completed\_audits / audit\_starts

```



Tracks:



\* UX clarity

\* form complexity

\* loading speed

\* perceived value



Target:



\* 70%+



\---



\## 3. Share Rate



Formula:



```txt id="6d8k8m"

shared\_reports / completed\_audits

```



Tracks:



\* virality

\* perceived usefulness

\* emotional impact of results



This is one of the strongest signals for organic growth.



Target:



\* 10–20%



\---



\## 4. Lead Capture Rate



Formula:



```txt id="a2hj1t"

emails\_collected / completed\_audits

```



Tracks:



\* trust

\* value perception

\* conversion intent



Target:



\* 20–30%



\---



\## 5. Consultation Request Rate



Formula:



```txt id="2j0d7r"

consultation\_requests / completed\_audits

```



Tracks:



\* purchase intent

\* seriousness of savings opportunity



Target:



\* 5–10%
