\# ECONOMICS



\# Estimated Lead Value



Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits to startups already spending heavily on AI tooling.



A qualified lead in this market is valuable because:



\* switching costs are relatively low

\* AI spend is recurring

\* startup tooling budgets grow quickly



Assumption:



\* average converted company spends $2,000/month on AI tools

\* Credex captures \~10% effective margin through discounted credits and vendor arbitrage



Estimated monthly gross profit per customer:



\* \~$200/month



Estimated yearly value:



\* \~$2400/customer/year



If the average customer remains active for 12–18 months, estimated lifetime value (LTV) becomes:



\* \~$3000–$4500

\# Biggest Economic Risks



\## 1. AI pricing changes rapidly



Vendors may reduce prices or bundle features, reducing optimization opportunities.



\## 2. Trust barrier



Financial recommendations require strong credibility.



Poor audit accuracy would immediately damage trust.



\## 3. Low-frequency usage



Most teams will not audit spend weekly.



Retention may depend more on alerts, benchmarks, and periodic monitoring than daily usage.
